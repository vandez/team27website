Net-a-Porter VR Project

Our Project

We are creating a virtual wardrobe in augmented reality using the Hololens in collaboration with Net-a-Porter. Our aim is to bring the EIP (Extremely Important Person) experience to everyone, where customers will be able to try on a selected clothing virtually and see how it fits them.
